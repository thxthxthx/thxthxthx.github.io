Star Wars was released 40 years ago on the 25th of May 1997.
I think the best hommage made to this film did not happen this year, or this month, as the anniversary date came closer.
In fact, the best hommage made to the original Star Wars film happened last year in Januray 2016 when Tom F released his modern trailer on YouTube.
This modern trailer uses the musical score from The Force Awakens' trailer. The fact that such a strong fan made trailer edit of a 40 year film can be so strong and powerful just confirms how modern Star Wars was, how modern Star Wars still is.

This modern trailer for Star Wars is fact the second that Tom F made for the saga. His first Star Wars modern trailer was in fact for The Empire Strikes Back and it feels even better!
